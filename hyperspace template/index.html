The Internet

The Internet - once the pinnacle of human evolution, now just a vast array of pornography and advertisements.

  • It is a Global Information System.
  • Logically linked together by a globally unique space address based on the Internet Protocol.
  • Is able to support communications using the Transmission Control Protocol/Internet Protocol (TCP/IP) suite or its subsequent.
  • Provides, uses, or makes accessible, either publicly or privately, high level services layered on the communications and related infrastructure described.

History of the Internet

  • 1969 – the year internet was born. Back then it was called the "ARPANET"
  • 1972 – email was introduced
  • 1989 – the World Wide Web (WWW) was introduced by Sir Tim Berners-Lee at CERN, Switzerland. It originally was intended for scientists, but it has become the main medium in accessing the Internet.

Types of Interconnection

  • Wired – actual physical connection
  • Wireless – via electromagnetic signals
